About

Lily Dokhanchi

I’m a writer and reporter based in Chicago with a passion for storytelling and making complex topics accessible for all readers. I am currently pursuing my Master of Science in Journalism at Northwestern's Medill School in the Magazine specialty. My work spans profiles, narrative features, interviews, science and health communication. I bring experience in writing, reporting, copyediting and packaging stories across text, audio and visual formats. Check out my work below and feel free to contact me — I'd love to connect.

Beats/Interests: Culture & fashion, profiles, science/health, education policy, current events and political news.
